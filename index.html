Portfolio

Dendra

Dendra is a cyberinfrastructure project for real-time sensor data storage, retrieval, management, and curation. It is a cloud-based, multi-organizational system, designed to support massive permanent monitoring efforts.

Strykr

Strykr is a martial arts training platform for all ages and disciplines. From the casual kickboxer to the pro taekwondoist, Strykr helps you improve your game. New Leaf Solutions is a member of the design and development team for this smart fitness product.

Vizible

Twizgrid

Me+My
